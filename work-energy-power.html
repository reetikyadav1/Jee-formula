Work, Energy & Power – JEE Practice

1. Work done by a force is:

Solution: W = F · s cosθ, dot product.

2. Kinetic energy K =:

Solution: K = (1/2) mv².

3. Work-energy theorem states:

Solution: Net work = change in kinetic energy.

4. Conservative force:

Solution: Work is path-independent.

5. Potential energy U for gravity is:

Solution: U = mgh (taking U=0 at h=0).

6. Power P is:

Solution: P = W/t = F · v.

7. Mechanical energy is conserved if:

Solution: K + U = constant for conservative forces.

8. Elastic potential energy U =:

Solution: U = (1/2) k x² for spring.

9. Average power is:

Solution: P_avg = W / t.

10. Work done against gravity is:

Solution: W = mgh (lifting against gravity).

11. Non-conservative force example:

Solution: Friction dissipates energy, path-dependent.

12. Total mechanical energy E =:

Solution: E = K + U.

13. Power in rotational motion is:

Solution: P = τ ω.

14. Work done by variable force:

Solution: W = ∫ F · dx.
