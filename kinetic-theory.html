Kinetic Theory – JEE Practice

1. Ideal gas equation PV =:

Solution: PV = nRT.

2. RMS speed v_rms =:

Solution: v_rms = √(3RT/M).

3. Degrees of freedom f for monatomic gas:

Solution: f = 3 (translational).

4. Mean free path λ =:

Solution: λ = 1/(√2 π d² n).

5. Average kinetic energy per molecule =:

Solution: (3/2) kT for ideal gas.

6. Pressure P =:

Solution: P = (1/3) ρ v_rms².

7. For diatomic gas, f =:

Solution: f = 5 (3 translational + 2 rotational).

8. Gas constant R =:

Solution: R = 8.314 J/mol·K.

9. Boltzmann constant k =:

Solution: k = R / N_A.

10. Most probable speed v_mp =:

Solution: v_mp = √(2RT/M).

11. Average speed v_avg =:

Solution: v_avg = √(8RT/πM).

12. Internal energy U for ideal gas =:

Solution: U = (f/2) nRT.

13. Collision frequency z =:

Solution: z = √2 π d² n v.

14. Avogadro's number N_A =:

Solution: N_A = 6
