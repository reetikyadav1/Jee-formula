JEE Physics Numerical Mock – 2005

Total Questions: 15 | Time: 60 minutes | All questions are numerical answer type | Assume g = 10 m/s² unless stated | Calculators not allowed

Time Left: 60:00

1. A particle moves with v = 2t² m/s. Find displacement in 2 s from rest. (Answer in meters)

Solution: v = ds/dt = 2t², ds = 2t² dt, s = ∫0^2 2t² dt = (2/3)t³ from 0 to 2 = (2/3)*8 = 16/3 ≈ 5.33 m.

2. Spring k=200 N/m, mass 1 kg, find frequency. (Answer in Hz)

Solution: f = 1/(2π) √(k/m) = 1/(2*3.14) √(200/1) ≈ 1/6.28 * 14.14 ≈ 2.25 Hz.

3. Atwood machine, m1=4 kg, m2=2 kg, find acceleration. (Answer in m/s²)

Solution: a = (m1 - m2)g / (m1 + m2) = (4-2)*10 / (4+2) = 20/6 ≈ 3.33 m/s².

4. Ball dropped from 20 m, find velocity at 10 m. (Answer in m/s)

Solution: v² = u² + 2gh = 0 + 2*10*10 = 200, v=14.14 m/s.

5. Torque on rod 2 m, force 10 N at 60° to rod. Find τ. (Answer in Nm)

Solution: τ = r F sinθ = 2 * 10 * sin60 = 20 * 0.866 ≈ 17.32 Nm.

6. Work done by gravity on 5 kg block sliding 10 m down incline 30°. (Answer in J)

Solution: W = mgh = 5*10*10*sin30 = 50*5 = 250 J.

7. Gas at 27°C, volume doubled at constant P, find T. (Answer in K)

Solution: V/T = const, 2V / T = V/300, T=600 K.

8. Heat capacity at constant V for 2 mol monoatomic gas. (Answer in J/K)

Solution: C_V = (3/2) nR = 1.5 * 2 * 8.314 ≈ 24.94 J/K.

9. Efficiency of Carnot engine T1=600 K, T2=300 K. (Answer as fraction)

Solution: η = 1 - T2/T1 = 1 - 0.5 = 0.5.

10. Resistance 5 Ω, voltage 10 V, find power. (Answer in W)

Solution: P = V²/R = 100/5 = 20 W.

11. Capacitor 4 μF charged to 20 V, energy stored. (Answer in μJ)

Solution: U = (1/2) C V² = 0.5 * 4e-6 * 400 = 0.8e-3 J = 800 μJ.

12. Wire 1 m, B=0.5 T, I=2 A, force on wire. (Answer in N)

Solution: F = I l B sin90 = 2*1*0.5 = 1 N.

13. Potential difference across 10 Ω in 20 V circuit. (Answer in V)

Solution: V = IR, but need total R. Assuming series, V=20 V.

14. Electron accelerated by 100 V, find speed. (Answer in m/s)

Solution: (1/2)mv² = eV, v = √(2eV/m) ≈ √(2*1.6e-19*100 / 9.1e-31) ≈ 5.9e6 m/s.

15. Radioactive decay, N0=1000, λ=0.1 s^-1, N after 2 s. (Answer)

Solution: N = N0 e^-λt = 1000 * e^-0.2 ≈ 818.
