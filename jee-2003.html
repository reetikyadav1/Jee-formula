JEE Physics Numerical Mock – 2003

Total Questions: 15 | Time: 60 minutes | All questions are numerical answer type | Assume g = 10 m/s² unless stated | Calculators not allowed

Time Left: 60:00

1. A particle moves in a circle of radius 2 m with constant tangential acceleration 3 m/s². If initial speed is 4 m/s, find the time to complete one revolution. (Answer in seconds)

Solution: Use v² = u² + 2a_s, but for tangential, a_t = dv/dt. Time for one rev: θ = 2π, ω = √(ω0² + 2αθ), t = (√(ω² - ω0²))/α. ω² = ω0² + 2αθ ⇒ ω² = 16 + 2*3*2π ⇒ ω² = 16 + 12π ⇒ ω = √(16 + 37.7) ≈ 6.8 rad/s. t = 2π / ω ≈ 0.92 s.

2. A block of mass 2 kg slides down a rough incline of 30° with μ = 0.2. Find the acceleration. (Answer in m/s²)

Solution: mg sinθ - μ mg cosθ = ma ⇒ 20*0.5 - 0.2*20*0.866 = 2a ⇒ 10 - 3.46 = 2a ⇒ a = 3.27 m/s².

3. Two masses m1=1 kg, m2=2 kg connected by string over pulley. Find acceleration. (Answer in m/s²)

Solution: (m2 - m1)g / (m1 + m2) = (2-1)*10 / (1+2) = 10/3 ≈ 3.33 m/s².

4. Projectile fired at 30° with speed 20 m/s. Find range. (Answer in meters)

Solution: R = u² sin2θ / g = 400 * sin60 / 10 = 400 * 0.866 / 10 = 34.64 m.

5. A rod of length 1 m pivoted at end, falls from vertical. Find angular acceleration at start. (Answer in rad/s²)

Solution: τ = I α, τ = mg (l/2) sinθ, I = ml²/3, α = (3g/2l) sinθ, at θ=0, α=0, but for small θ, α = 3g/(2l) ≈ 15 rad/s².

6. Elastic collision, m1=2 kg at 4 m/s, m2=1 kg at rest. Find v1 after collision. (Answer in m/s)

Solution: v1 = (m1-m2)/(m1+m2) u1 = (2-1)/(2+1)*4 = 4/3 ≈ 1.33 m/s.

7. Ideal gas at 300 K, pressure doubled at constant volume. Find final T. (Answer in K)

Solution: P/T = constant, 2P / T = P/300, T=600 K.

8. RMS speed of gas at 27°C. Find v_rms. (Answer in m/s, R=8.314, M=0.032 kg/mol for O2)

Solution: v_rms = √(3RT/M) = √(3*8.314*300/0.032) ≈ 483 m/s.

9. Heat added to 1 mol monoatomic gas at constant volume, ΔU = 1500 J. Find ΔT. (Answer in K)

Solution: ΔU = (3/2) nR ΔT, 1500 = (3/2)*8.314*ΔT, ΔT = 120.5 K.

10. Capacitor 10 μF charged to 100 V, connected to 5 μF. Find final V. (Answer in V)

Solution: Q = C1 V1 = 10e-6 *100 = 1e-3 C. C_eq = 15e-6, V = Q/C_eq = 66.67 V.

11. Current in 2 Ω resistor in circuit with 10 V battery and 3 Ω in series. Find I. (Answer in A)

Solution: R_total = 5 Ω, I = V/R = 10/5 = 2 A.

12. Magnetic field at center of loop radius 0.1 m, I=5 A. Find B. (Answer in T)

Solution: B = μ0 I / (2r) = 4πe-7 *5 / 0.2 = 3.14e-5 T.

13. Force between two charges 1 μC each, 1 m apart. Find F. (Answer in N)

Solution: F = k q1 q2 / r² = 9e9 *1e-12 /1 = 9e-3 N.

14. Photon energy for λ=500 nm. Find E. (Answer in eV)

Solution: E = hc/λ = 1240 / 500 = 2.48 eV.

15. Stopping potential for metal φ=2 eV, λ=400 nm. Find V_s. (Answer in V)

Solution: hc/λ = φ + eV_s, 1240/400 = 2 + V_s, 3.1 = 2 + V_s, V_s=1.1 V.
