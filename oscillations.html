Oscillations – JEE Practice

1. In SHM, acceleration a =:

Solution: a = -ω² x.

2. Time period T for spring-mass system:

Solution: T = 2π √(m/k).

3. For simple pendulum, T =:

Solution: T = 2π √(l/g).

4. Total energy in SHM is:

Solution: E = (1/2) k A² = (1/2) m ω² A².

5. Kinetic energy at mean position:

Solution: K_max at mean position.

6. Potential energy at extreme position:

Solution: U_max at extreme position.

7. Angular frequency ω =:

Solution: ω = 2π / T.

8. Displacement x = A sin(ωt + φ). φ is:

Solution: φ is initial phase.

9. For spring, force constant k =:

Solution: k = m ω².

10. In damped oscillation, amplitude:

Solution: Amplitude decreases exponentially.

11. Frequency f =:

Solution: f = 1/T = ω / 2π.

12. Velocity in SHM v =:

Solution: v = ω √(A² - x²).

13. Restoring force F =:

Solution: F = -k x.

14. In SHM, x = A cos ωt. At t=0, x =:

Solution: x(0) = A.

15. Energy conservation in SHM:

Solution: Total energy E = K + U = constant.
