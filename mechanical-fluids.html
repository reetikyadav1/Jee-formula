Mechanical Properties of Fluids – JEE Practice

1. Pressure in a fluid is:

Solution: P = F/A.

2. Pascal's law states:

Solution: Pressure applied to enclosed fluid is transmitted equally.

3. Bernoulli's theorem relates:

Solution: P + (1/2)ρv² + ρgh = constant.

4. Viscosity is:

Solution: Viscosity opposes relative motion between layers.

5. Surface tension is:

Solution: Surface tension = force/length = energy/area.

6. In a Venturi meter, pressure is:

Solution: Velocity higher, pressure lower at constriction.

7. Coefficient of viscosity η has units:

Solution: η = F/(A dv/dx), unit Pa·s.

8. Capillary rise h =:

Solution: h = 2T cosθ /(ρ g r).

9. Reynolds number Re =:

Solution: Re = ρ v L / η.

10. Hydraulic lift works on:

Solution: Pressure transmitted equally in fluids.

11. Stoke's law F =:

Solution: F = 6πη r v for sphere.

12. Terminal velocity is:

Solution: When drag = weight, velocity constant.

13. Angle of contact for water is:

Solution: Acute angle for wetting liquids.

14. Bernoulli's equation assumes:

Solution: Ideal fluid, steady, incompressible, non-viscous.
