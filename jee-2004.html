JEE Physics Numerical Mock – 2004

Total Questions: 15 | Time: 60 minutes | All questions are numerical answer type | Assume g = 10 m/s² unless stated | Calculators not allowed

Time Left: 60:00

1. A car accelerates from rest at 2 m/s² for 10 s, then decelerates at 1 m/s² to stop. Find total distance. (Answer in meters)

Solution: First phase: s1 = (1/2)at² = 0.5*2*100 = 100 m, v=20 m/s. Second: v²= u² + 2as, 0=400 + 2*(-1)s2, s2=200 m. Total s=300 m.

2. Block on incline 45°, μ=0.3, find minimum force to pull up. (Answer in N, m=5 kg)

Solution: F = mg(sinθ + μ cosθ) = 50*(0.707 + 0.3*0.707) ≈ 50*1.01 ≈ 50.5 N.

3. Two springs k1=100 N/m, k2=200 N/m in series, find effective k. (Answer in N/m)

Solution: 1/k_eff = 1/100 + 1/200 = 0.015, k_eff ≈ 66.67 N/m.

4. Ball thrown vertically up with 20 m/s, find time to reach max height. (Answer in seconds)

Solution: v = u - gt, 0=20 - 10t, t=2 s.

5. Rod length 2 m, mass 4 kg, pivoted at center, find I about end. (Answer in kg m²)

Solution: I_cm = ml²/12 = 4*4/12 = 1.33, parallel axis: I = 1.33 + 4*(1)² = 5.33 kg m².

6. Inelastic collision, m1=3 kg at 6 m/s, m2=2 kg at 2 m/s, find common v. (Answer in m/s)

Solution: (m1 u1 + m2 u2)/(m1+m2) = (18 + 4)/5 = 4.4 m/s.

7. Gas expands isothermally, work done 1000 J, find ΔU. (Answer in J)

Solution: For isothermal, ΔU = 0.

8. Mean free path λ = 10^-7 m, d=3×10^-10 m, find n. (Answer in m^-3)

Solution: λ = 1/(√2 π d² n), n = 1/(√2 π d² λ) ≈ 2.5×10^25 m^-3.

9. Adiabatic process, γ=1.4, P1=2 atm, V1=1 L, V2=2 L, find P2. (Answer in atm)

Solution: P V^γ = const, P2 = P1 (V1/V2)^γ = 2*(0.5)^1.4 ≈ 0.76 atm.

10. Capacitors 2 μF and 3 μF in parallel, find C_eq. (Answer in μF)

Solution: C_eq = 2 + 3 = 5 μF.

11. Resistor 4 Ω, inductor 0.1 H, find time constant. (Answer in seconds)

Solution: τ = L/R = 0.1/4 = 0.025 s.

12. Current loop area 0.01 m², B=0.1 T, torque max when θ=90°. Find τ_max. (Answer in Nm, I=2 A)

Solution: τ = N I A B sinθ, max = 1*2*0.01*0.1 = 0.002 Nm.

13. Drift velocity v_d = 10^-4 m/s, n=10^28 m^-3, e=1.6×10^-19, find J. (Answer in A/m²)

Solution: J = n e v_d = 10^28 * 1.6e-19 * 1e-4 = 1.6e7 A/m².

14. De Broglie wavelength for electron at 100 eV. Find λ. (Answer in nm)

Solution: λ = h / √(2 m E) ≈ 150 / √E (in pm), for 100 eV ≈ 0.123 nm.

15. Half-life 10 years, initial 100 g, find mass after 30 years. (Answer in g)

Solution: N = N0 (1/2)^(t/T) = 100 * (1/2)^3 = 12.5 g.
