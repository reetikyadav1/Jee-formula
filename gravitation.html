Gravitation – JEE Practice

1. Newton's law of gravitation F =:

Solution: F = GMm/r².

2. Gravitational field strength E =:

Solution: E = GM/r².

3. Gravitational potential V =:

Solution: V = -GM/r.

4. Orbital velocity of satellite v =:

Solution: v = √(GM/r).

5. Escape velocity v_esc =:

Solution: v_esc = √(2GM/r).

6. Time period of satellite T =:

Solution: T = 2π √(r³/GM).

7. Kepler's third law T² ∝:

Solution: T² ∝ r³.

8. Gravitational potential energy U =:

Solution: U = -GMm/r.

9. Acceleration due to gravity g =:

Solution: g = GM/r².

10. Geostationary satellite height h =:

Solution: h ≈ 36000 km above Earth.

11. Gravitational constant G value is:

Solution: G = 6.67×10^-11 N m²/kg².

12. Weight of body in satellite is:

Solution: Weightlessness, apparent weight = 0.

13. Kepler's first law describes:

Solution: Planets move in ellipses.

14. Binding energy of satellite is:

Solution: Binding energy = -GMm/(2r).
